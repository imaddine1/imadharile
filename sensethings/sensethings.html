About SenseTings

SenseTings is an innovative startup that is addressing the important issue on security and authenticity in the digital and physical documents. T3 technology, which stands for Trust, Truck, and Trace, is a comprehensive solution for creating , securing, and verifying phygital (physical and digital) documents.
