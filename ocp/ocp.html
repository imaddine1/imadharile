About Ocp

OCP Group is a global leader in the phosphate industry. It specializes in mining, processing, and exporting phosphate and phosphate-based products, such as fertilizers, to support sustainable agriculture worldwide. Renowned for its innovation and commitment to environmental and social impact, OCP plays a vital role in global food security
